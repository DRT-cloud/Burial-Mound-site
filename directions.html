// Location & Directions

PAWNEE, OKLAHOMA

42251 S 34500 Rd · Four Gates · GPS for Every Entrance

All Burial Mound events are held at The Burial Mound Event Center — a private 270-acre range east of Pawnee, Oklahoma, spanning two properties about a mile apart. There are four entrance gates. Pick the one closest to your approach, or follow the event-specific gate instructions sent at registration.
