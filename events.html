WHAT TO EXPECT

A walkthrough of race day at The Burial Mound.

01

Check-In

Arrive early, sign waivers, confirm registration, pick up your bib number and t-shirt. Check-in typically opens at 7:00 AM.

02

Safety Brief

Mandatory safety briefing at 8:00 AM. Course overview, rules of engagement, stage descriptions, and emergency procedures.

03

Run & Shoot

Competitors released individually every 5 minutes. Run the course, engage targets at each shooting stage, and push through physical obstacles.

04

Awards & Community

Results posted, awards ceremony, sponsor prize drawings, and a meal included with entry. Share stories and connect with the community.
