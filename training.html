TRAINING PROGRAMS

From one-on-one instruction to full-scale law enforcement exercises, we build training around your goals.

One-on-One

Private Instruction

Personalized one-on-one sessions tailored to your skill level and goals. Whether you're refining fundamentals or preparing for competition, we customize every session to your needs. Rifle, pistol, or combined arms training on world-class terrain.

Teams of 4–20

Group Training

Team-based exercises for groups of 4–20 participants. Build communication, coordination, and marksmanship under realistic conditions across rolling hills, wooded draws, and open fields. Ideal for clubs, teams, and experienced shooting groups.

LE / Military

Law Enforcement

LE qualification courses and scenario-based training on 250+ acres of diverse terrain. Night operations, vehicle stages, structures, and dynamic shooting positions. Designed to meet department qualification standards and build real-world skills.

Team Building

Corporate Team Building

Unique outdoor team experiences that build trust, communication, and leadership. No corporate ropes course — this is the real thing. Customizable events on world-class competition terrain with professional instruction and safety oversight.

WHAT TO BRING

Minimum gear requirements for training and competition at The Burial Mound.

Rifle

Centerfire rifle with sling recommended

Pistol

Centerfire pistol with holster

Magazines & Ammo

Loaded magazines. Plan 2x minimum round count.

Eye & Ear Protection

Safety glasses and hearing protection required

Water

Hydration pack or water bottles — stay hydrated

Running Shoes

Trail runners recommended for uneven terrain

Cell Phone

For emergency communication on the property
