BORN FROM THE LAND

Built on 250 acres of rolling Oklahoma terrain, The Burial Mound is where shooters come to test themselves against the land, the clock, and each other. From dawn Run N Guns through wooded draws and open fields to our signature Twilight Biathlon under starlit skies — this is competition the way it was meant to be. Raw. Real. Relentless.
