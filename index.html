(About)

The Burial Mound isn't just a shooting range. It's a proving ground where runners become shooters and shooters become athletes. We built this place because we believe competition should push you past your limits — physically, mentally, and technically. Every course is designed to test what you're made of. Every match is a chance to find out who you really are when the clock is running and the targets are waiting.
